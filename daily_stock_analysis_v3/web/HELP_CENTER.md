## 📖 Understanding Reports

### What does "BUY" mean?
The AI thinks it's a good time to buy this stock.

### What does "HOLD" mean?
If you own it, keep it. If not, wait for a better price.

### What does "SELL" mean?
Consider selling if you own it. Don't buy if you don't.

### What are "Action Levels"?
Exact prices to use:
- **Ideal Buy**: Best price to buy
- **Stop Loss**: Sell if it drops to this (limits losses)
- **Take Profit**: Sell when it reaches this (takes profits)

### What do the checkmarks mean?
- ✅ = Good sign
- ⚠️ = Be careful
- ❌ = Bad sign

### How accurate is the AI?
The AI analyzes millions of data points, but:
- It's not perfect
- It can't predict the future
- Always do your own research too

## 🔧 Technical Issues

### The page won't load
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if still not working

### Analysis is taking too long
1. Wait up to 30 seconds (complex analysis)
2. Refresh and try again
3. Check if you have API calls remaining
4. Contact support if still slow

### I can't login
1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Login with new password

### The report looks weird
1. Refresh the page
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact support with screenshot
