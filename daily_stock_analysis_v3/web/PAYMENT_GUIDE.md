## ❓ Payment FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: When will I be charged?**  
**A**: Immediately when you subscribe. Then on the same date each month.

**Q: Can I change plans later?**  
**A**: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: What if I want to cancel?**  
**A**: Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel. No questions asked.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
**A**: Yes! 7-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers.

### Card Payments

**Q: Is my card information secure?**  
**A**: Yes! We use Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 compliant). We never see your card number.

**Q: What if my card is declined?**  
**A**: Check:
- Card is not expired
- Sufficient funds
- International transactions enabled
- Contact your bank if still declined

**Q: Can I save my card?**  
**A**: Yes! Your card is saved securely for automatic monthly billing.

### Crypto Payments

**Q: What if I send the wrong amount?**  
**A**: We'll contact you to arrange refund or additional payment.

**Q: How many confirmations are needed?**  
**A**: 
- Bitcoin: 1 confirmation (~10 min)
- Ethereum: 3 confirmations (~5 min)
- USDT/USDC: 12 confirmations (~3 min)

**Q: What if the price changes while I'm paying?**  
**A**: The amount is locked for 15 minutes. Send within that time.

**Q: Can I get a refund in crypto?**  
**A**: Yes, refunds are sent to the same wallet address.

### Singapore Payments

**Q: Is PayNow safe?**  
**A**: Yes! PayNow is regulated by MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore).

**Q: What if I send to wrong UEN?**  
**A**: Contact us immediately at support@dailystockanalysis.com

**Q: Can I use CPF?**  
**A**: No, CPF cannot be used for investment services.

### Billing Questions

**Q: What currency do you charge in?**  
**A**: USD (US Dollars). Your bank converts automatically.

**Q: Are there hidden fees?**  
**A**: No! The price you see is what you pay.

**Q: Do you charge GST?**  
**A**: For Singapore customers, 9% GST is added at checkout.

**Q: Can I get an invoice?**  
**A**: Yes! Invoices are emailed automatically. Download anytime from Settings.

**Q: What if I'm charged twice?**  
**A**: Email billing@dailystockanalysis.com. We'll refund immediately.
