## 📊 Understanding Your Report

### Example Report (Simplified)

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║  APPLE INC. (AAPL)                           ║
║  Current Price: $150.00                      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║  🎯 RECOMMENDATION: BUY                      ║
║                                              ║
║  💰 ACTION LEVELS:                           ║
║  • Ideal Buy: $148.00                        ║
║  • Stop Loss: $142.00                        ║
║  • Take Profit: $165.00                      ║
║                                              ║
║  ✅ CHECKLIST:                               ║
║  ✓ Good trend                                ║
║  ✓ Reasonable price                          ║
║  ✓ Good volume                               ║
║  ⚠ Watch: Competition risk                   ║
║                                              ║
║  📰 NEWS: Positive (3 recent articles)       ║
║  😊 SENTIMENT: Optimistic                    ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### What Each Section Means

**🎯 Recommendation**: What the AI thinks you should do
- **BUY** = Good time to buy
- **HOLD** = Keep if you own, don't buy yet
- **SELL** = Consider selling

**💰 Action Levels**: Exact prices to use
- **Ideal Buy**: Best price to buy
- **Stop Loss**: Sell if it drops to this (limits losses)
- **Take Profit**: Sell when it reaches this (takes profits)

**✅ Checklist**: Why this recommendation
- ✓ = Good sign
- ⚠ = Be careful
- ❌ = Bad sign

**📰 News**: Recent news about the company
**😊 Sentiment**: Whether news is positive or negative
